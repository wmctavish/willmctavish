- The How & The Why -

There may well be a kind of hubris in having a "philosophy" so early in one's journey as a developer. And yet, we've all been online - we know what delights us, and what repels us. The user experience on a site or app can be compared to a joke: if you have to explain it, you've failed.

Where time and attention are the prime currencies, intuitive design is invaluable. Keep it fast, light, and coherent.
